Outreach Trip

We love to host short-term outreach teams.

If you, your church, or a group, would like to serve in any way in Nepal then we would love to talk with you about the many opportunities here. Short-term outreaches offer visiting teams the opportunity to gain cross-cultural ministry experience, and they also provides us with fresh impetus for our ongoing work here.

We can host teams of up to 20 people on-site in accommodation that is 'dorm-style' (bunk beds and shared bathrooms). We also have two rooms with queen sized beds available for married couples.

We have various opportunities across many districts of Nepal so we can tailor an outreach to what you feel led to and gifted to engage in. We develop the outreach schedule in partnership and then we make all the arrangements for your trip and accompany you throughout.
