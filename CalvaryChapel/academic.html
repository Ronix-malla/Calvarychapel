Programs on Offer:

Our main program is a 2-year 'Diploma in Theology.' For Nepali students, the type of diploma available depends on your previous education history and length of study with us. You can gain a full diploma, a certificate of completion, or simply audit classes.

To graduate from Calvary Chapel Bible College Nepal with a full 2-year "Diploma in Theology" each student must:

1.

Have passed high-school class 10 (SEE)

2.

Complete a minimum of 80 credits with course grades at C or higher

3.

Maintain 90% attendance

4.

Participate in an evangelistic outreach each semester

5.

Fulfill mandatory hours of servanthood

Academic Programs and Certificates

Diploma in Theology:

Students that meet the requirements above graduate with a 'Deploma in Theology' (1-year or 2-year). This diploma is issued internally from CCBCN.

Certificate of Completion:

Students that do not have a high school diploma (in Nepal, those that have not passed class 10, SEE), but fulfill all the other requirements listed above, will graduate with a 'Certificate of Completion' from CCBCN (1-year or 2-year).

Audit Program:

Auditing is for a student who cannot attend CCBCN full-time nor meet the requirements above but still desires to study for personal enrichment. Audit students can select and attend classes as they wish. A certificate of 'AU' can be issued if a student attends the equivalent of 2-semesters worth of classes. Audit students do not have to complete assignments nor take tests, however, they are encouraged to do so for personal benefit.

Affiliation:

Calvary Chapel Bible College Nepal is affiliated with the main Calvary Chapel Bible College located in California, USA. As an affiliate campus, Calvary Chapel Bible College Nepal is under the same graduation requirements, standards, and policy guidelines as the main campus. Our affiliation with CCBC affirms the credibility of our program.
