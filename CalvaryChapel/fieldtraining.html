Field Training

Our On-The-Field trainings are ongoing in various locations around Nepal. These courses are developed alongside and tailored to the needs of local church leaders. They generally run over 1 or 2 years and consist of 1 week long trainings scheduled at regular intervals throughout the year.

Although the courses are customized to the needs of the local churches. generally the subjects we cover are

01
Inductive Bible Study

How to exegetically study the Bible.

02
OT Survey

Understanding and interpreting the OT

03
NT Survey

Understanding and interpreting the NT

04
Basic Biblical Doctrines

An overview of key Christian doctrines.

05
Homiletics

Expositional preaching and how to effectively disciple from the pulpit.

06
Disciple Making

How to encourage a disciple making culture

07
Pastoral Epistles

A verse by verse study through the Pastoral Epistles.

08
Biblical Leadership

A study of servant-leadership in the Bible

09
Worship in the Church

Worship leading, gifts of the Spirit in the church

10
Church Governance, Finances and Accountability

Practical training on structure and good practice in church leadership

11
Christian Apologetics, World Religions and Cults

Introducing basic apologetic arguments and different world views.

12
Church History

A short overview of CH with a focus on the CH of Nepal
