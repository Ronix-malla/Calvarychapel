International Student Information

As a Calvary Chapel Bible College affiliate campus we can accommodate international students moving between Calvary Chapel campuses. Transferable credits will need to meet the specifications of Calvary Chapel Bible College USA and will require some accommodation on our behalf. If you are considering taking a semester with us as part of an ongoing CCBC degree, please Contact us. to find out which classes will be taught in English and to discuss if we are able to provide the particular classes you require.

If you are not currently studying at a Calvary Chapel campus but want to study the Bible at an Evangelical College while also gaining experience in a cross-cultural environment, we would love to hear from you.

It’s a big decision to study abroad so we are available to connect with you over a video call and discuss things in greater detail.

After an initial contact, if we agree that CCBC Nepal might be right for you, then the application process is as follows:

Admission Process

Steps

Download and complete your application and complete your application and reference forms and email them to admin@ccbcnepal.com.

When submitting your application form you will need to pay a $25 application fee. Click here for online payment . Please also email us at admin@ccbcnepal.com to notify us of the payment

If your application is successfull, we will notify you by email. Upon acceptance. Your tuition fee will need to be paid in full before the semester start date (see 'Financial Information' below for further details).

We will advise you further on how to apply for a visa and what you will need to bring.

Financial Information

Our fees correspond to other extension campuses and are higher for international Students that Nepali Students (The fees for Nepalis are highly subsidized by scholarship donations.)

International Student

01

International Student

Fees: $2,000 USD / Semester (Included $25 application fee)

This includes the following items:

  • Tuition Fees
  • Housing and food
  • Wifi on campus
  • Airport pick-up & drop-off
  • Outreach trip

This amount does not include:

  • Airline Tickets
  • Travel visa
  • Medical expenses (insurance)
  • Personal Expenses (eating out, etc)
