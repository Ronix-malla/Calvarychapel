Admission Process

Steps

Contact Us. To discuss your interest in studying with us.

If the initial contact is positive, we’ll advise you to download and complete your application form. If you live local in the Kathmandu valley then please bring the application to the CCBCN campus so we can meet you in person. If you are based outside of the KTM valley, please send a scan or photo of your application to us at admin@ccbcnepal.com

On or before Orientation Day you will be required to pay the application fee and the first month’s tuition.
