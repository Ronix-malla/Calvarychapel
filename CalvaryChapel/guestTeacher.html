Guest Teacher

Get Involved / Guest Teacher

Teach with Us

We welcome guest speakers, who are experienced at exegetical teaching, to join us in Nepal to teach a block-class. Also, teachers who would like to contribute by teaching remotely through an online medium are also very welcome (either regular teaching or a one-off block class). Guest teachers are a huge blessing to our students and staff.

For guest teachers who come to Nepal, we have one room set aside on campus for visiting pastors or teachers (and their wives) that has a queen bed and an attached bathroom / shower. If there is more than one pastor or teacher staying on campus at the same time, we have other rooms available with twin size beds.

Accomodation and food for guest teachers on-campus is free of charge. The only out of pocket expenses are: airline tickets, visa costs, tourist excursions that you want pursue, and any additional outreaches that you may feel called to pursue while in-country.

For available dates and other information about serving as a guest teacher, please contact us.

Calvary Chapel Bible College Nepal is a two-year discipleship and Bible-training ministry. Our desire is to encourage Christians to grow deeper and go further in their relationships with Jesus. We aim to provide a Christ-centered and Bible-saturated environment that is conducive for both learning and serving. Our program is designed to teach students how to 'rightly divide the word of truth' and to equip them for 'the work of the ministry'.
